Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours in advance without any fees. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before may incur a charge.

Can I modify my reservation?

Yes, you can modify your reservation by contacting us directly or through your account on our website.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we take dietary restrictions seriously. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary needs when making your reservation.

What time should I arrive for my reservation?

We recommend arriving at least 10-15 minutes before your reservation time for a smooth dining experience.
